Temecula's first and finest tattoo studio Soul Expressions Tattoo opened in 1993 and continues to be the premier tattoo studio in the Inland Empire.


Soul Expressions Studios houses 7 tattoo artist. Each artist specializes in different styles of tattooing. Traditional, poly tribal, photo realism, black and grey, full color, and more. The shop has been standing strong for 25 years !! New artist, new talent- sane great shop!
